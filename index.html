F.A.Q

  • People from which background can participate?

    This summit is open to all and you don't need to have a specific background, although having technical, statistics /mathematics and management background may be advantageous.

  • Is it compulsory to participate in team or we can register individually?

    You can register individually as well as in team for the summit.

  • Is Data Science Summit online or offline?

    Considering the present circumstances and better outreach of the event ,Data Science Summit will be completely conducted in online mode.

  • Are there any certificates for the participants?

    Yes. Every participant will be awarded with the certificates according to the events he/she participated in.

  • How will I be judged throughout the summit?

    You will be assessed on the basis of your performance in various events by a group of experienced judges/mentors.

  • Will I we get an advisor/mentor for datathon?

    You will be provided with a mentor who will guide you during the datathon event.

  • Will I have to pay anything for attending the Webinars by experts?

    No. All the events are free of cost for you if you have registered for the Data Science Summit.

  • Can students from different colleges club together and form a team?

    Yes. Making a team of any kind is permitted. Please refer to the detailed rules and regulations on our website for further information.

  • Will I be provided with the recordings of the sessions by experts?

    Yes. If you wish, you can ask for the recordings post the session is taken. Still, it's not guaranteed due to copyright issues—we advise you to attend the event and sessions with apt focus and keep noting the information you find useful.
